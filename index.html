OFFERS A HELPING HAND TO THE WOMEN IN CRISIS.

St.Ann’s Sneha Bhavan renders its helping hand to the Women in crisis, who need support, understanding, safety and security. Every life is precious and it is to be valued , cared, protected , dignified and up held.

St.Ann’s Social Centre focus on Rural/ Urban Development.Under the People Led Empowerment project work with Farmers to promote Organic farming.In the village, development activities are carried out with the participation and leadership of the people themselves.
