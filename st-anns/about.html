About Us

The sisters of St Ann of Luzern, Central Province renders service to society through various ways such as educating children in schools and providing good physical health through our hospitals and other projects for the betterment of the society.

Our mission:

To enhance the human dignity of the less privileged through empowerment, liberation and transformation.
