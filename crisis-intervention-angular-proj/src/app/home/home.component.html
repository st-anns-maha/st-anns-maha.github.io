Offers a helping hand to the women in crisis

St. Ann's Sneha Bhavan Women's Shelter Home renders its helping hand to the women in crisis, who need support, understanding, safety and security. Life is precious and it is to be valued, cared, protected, dignified and up held.

We Aim at

  • Residential care for the Battered Women
  • Counselling
  • Medical care
  • Ensure the Rights of Women in distress
  • Skill training
  • Upliftment of women through Self-help Groups
  • Networking with other like-minded organizations
  • Legal help
  • Rehabilitation
  • Follow up
